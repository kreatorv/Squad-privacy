General

What is Squad?

Squad lets a Squad Lead post a job — a ride, babysitting, an errand, a chore, and more — to a private roster of trusted helpers (Squad Members). Everyone on the roster is notified, and the first to accept is hired. No public marketplace, no strangers.

How does payment work?

Squad does not process payments and never charges your card. You and the Squad Member agree on a price when the job is posted, and you settle up directly — cash, Venmo, Zelle, Cash App, PayPal, whatever you arrange. All payment happens outside the app; Squad only displays a member’s preferred method and handle so you know how to pay them.

Is my information private?

Jobs are only ever shown to people in your own squad. Squad does not sell your data or share it with advertisers. A home address typed as “Home” stays masked as “Home” to the roster until someone accepts the job. See our Privacy Policy for full detail.

How do notifications work?

When a relevant job is posted, members who offer that type of help get a push notification. Make sure notifications are enabled for Squad in your device Settings.

How do I delete my account?

Open Edit Profile in the app and tap “Delete account” at the bottom. This permanently erases your account and personal data and cannot be undone. For a Squad Lead, it also removes your squad, its jobs, and any child logins you created.

For Squad Leads

How do I add Squad Members?

On your home screen, find “Invite members” and share your squad’s invite code. Each person signs up as a Squad Member and enters that code to join your roster.

How do I post a job?

Tap “＋ New job,” pick the job type, then fill in the details — time, location, price, and any notes. Tap “Send to Squad” and everyone who offers that type of help is notified.

Who sees a job I post?

Only the Squad Members whose listed help types match the job. For example, a ride goes to members who drive; a chore goes to members who do chores. Members with no types set see everything.

What does “first to accept” mean?

The job goes out to everyone at once, and the first member to tap Accept is hired. The job then flips to “Filled” for everyone else automatically — there’s no bidding and no back-and-forth.

Can I edit or cancel a job?

Yes. Open the job to edit its details while it’s still open, or cancel it. If a member already accepted, cancelling notifies them.

How do I set up a ride for my child?

Add a child under “Manage children” — you create a simple username + PIN login for them. When you post a ride you can attach the child, and they get a read-only view showing who’s driving them, the car, and pickup/drop-off.

What do the member stats mean?

For each member you’ll see jobs completed, times cancelled, and total earned — drawn from your squad’s job history so you know who’s reliable.

For Squad Members

How do I join a squad?

Ask your Squad Lead for their invite code, then enter it under “Join a squad” on your home screen. You can belong to more than one squad.

How do I get jobs?

When a Squad Lead posts a job that matches the help types you offer, you get a push notification and it appears in your Job queue. Open it to see the details.

How do I set what kinds of jobs I get?

In Edit Profile, check the types of help you offer (driving, babysitting, errands, chores, pet care, etc.). You’ll only be notified about jobs that match.

How does accepting a job work?

Tap Accept on a job you want. The first member to accept is hired; if someone beats you to it, the job shows as already filled. You can also decline to stop seeing a job.

How and when do I get paid?

Payment happens outside the app, directly between you and the Squad Lead. Set your preferred method and handle (e.g. your Venmo) in Edit Profile so leads know how to pay you. Squad never handles the money.

Can I cancel a job after accepting?

Yes — open the job and cancel. The job re-opens to the rest of the squad and the Squad Lead is notified. Frequent cancellations show in your stats, so only accept what you can do.

How do I leave a squad?

Open the squad from “My squads” and tap Leave. You’ll stop receiving that squad’s jobs.
