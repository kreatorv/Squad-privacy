Squad — Privacy Policy

Written in plain language. This is not legal advice; please have it reviewed before relying on it.

Effective date: June 9, 2026
App: Squad
Provider: Squad (operated by Vanderyu)
Contact: KreatorVInc@gmail.com

Who this covers

Squad lets a Squad Lead (an adult, typically a parent or guardian) post jobs — rides, babysitting, errands, chores, dog-walking, house-sitting, and similar — to an approved roster of helpers (Squad Members). A simple, read-only companion view (Child) lets a child see who is driving them for an assigned ride. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your choices.

Information we collect

From Squad Leads: your name, email address, an optional profile photo, an optional home address (used for a "Home" shortcut when posting rides), the squad and any children you set up, and the details of jobs you post — including addresses you type in, the price you offer, times, and notes.

From Squad Members: your name, email address, a confirmation that you are at least 13 years old, an optional profile photo, the types of help you offer (e.g. driving, babysitting, pet-sitting), your preferred payment method and handle (for example a Venmo username), and — if you offer driving — your vehicle's make, model, color, and an optional photo of the car. We also record your job activity (which jobs you accept, complete, or cancel).

For Children: a Squad Lead creates the child's account. We collect only a first name and sign-in credentials (a username and PIN the adult sets). We do not collect a child's email, photo, or any other personal details, and the Child view is read-only.

Automatically: a device push-notification token, so we can alert you to relevant jobs.

We do not collect GPS or background location. Any addresses are simply text a Squad Lead types into a job.

How we use information

  • To run the core service: posting jobs, routing each job to the Squad Members who offer that type of help, recording who accepts/completes/cancels, and showing a Squad Lead simple statistics about their members.
  • To show a Child the driver's name, photo, and car (and pickup/drop-off) for their accepted ride.
  • To send job-related push notifications (new jobs, re-opened jobs, updates, cancellations).

We do not process payments. The payment method and handle you provide are stored only so a Squad Lead knows how you wish to be paid; any actual payment happens outside the app.

How information is shared

  • Within your squad. A Squad Lead can see their Members' profile details (photo, vehicle, payment handle) and job statistics. Members see the job details a Squad Lead posts. A home address typed as "Home" is shown to other members only as "Home" until a member accepts the job; the actual address is then shown to the member doing the job (and to the assigned child for a ride).
  • A Child sees only the driver's name, photo, and car for their own accepted ride.
  • We do not sell personal information or share it with advertisers.
  • Service providers. We use Supabase (database, authentication, and file storage) to run the app and Expo's push service to deliver notifications. These providers process data on our behalf. We may also disclose information where required by law.

Children's privacy

Squad Members must confirm they are at least 13 years old; an account cannot be created without it. Child accounts are created and controlled by a Squad Lead (the consenting adult) and are limited to the minimal information described above — a first name and a login, no photo. If you believe a child's information has been collected improperly, contact us and we will delete it.

Data retention and deletion

We keep your information while your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated personal information at any time by emailing us. Aggregate, de-identified job history may be retained for a squad's statistics.

Security

We protect your information with industry-standard measures, including encrypted connections and database access controls (row-level security). No system is perfectly secure, but we work to keep your data safe.

Your choices

  • Edit or remove your profile photo, the help types you offer, payment details, vehicle info, or home address at any time in the app (Edit Profile).
  • Leave a squad at any time from your squad's screen.
  • Turn push notifications on or off in your device settings.
  • Request access to or deletion of your data via the contact email.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. We will post the new effective date here and, where appropriate, notify you in the app.
